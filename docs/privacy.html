Motif Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

Motif is a client for self-hosted remote development sessions. We do not sell personal information, show ads, or use third-party tracking or analytics SDKs. Most app data stays on your device or is sent only to the Motif server that you choose to connect to.

Information You Provide

Motif stores the server details you add, such as server name, server URL or host, port, transport type, and bearer token. This information is used to connect the app to your own Motif server. It is stored on your device and may be sent to the server you configure when you connect.

Terminal input, commands, file operations, uploaded attachments, session names, and related workspace data are transmitted to the Motif server you choose. The app developer does not receive this data unless you connect to a server operated by the developer for testing or review.

Camera

Motif uses the camera only to scan a pairing QR code for adding a server. QR code scanning is performed to extract the pairing link. Motif does not use the camera for advertising, tracking, or analytics.

Photos and Files

If you choose to attach an image or file from your device, Motif uploads the selected item to the active Motif server and inserts the uploaded path into the terminal. Motif does not access your photo library or files except for items you explicitly select.

Microphone and Voice Input

Motif uses the microphone only when you start voice input in the terminal composer. Audio is streamed to a speech recognition service to convert speech into text. The resulting text is inserted into the terminal input area so you can edit or send it. Motif does not use microphone audio for advertising or tracking.

Push Notifications

If push notifications are enabled, Motif registers your device with Apple Push Notification service and sends the push token to the Motif server you are connected to. Notification payloads are encrypted by your server and decrypted on your device where supported. Motif stores notification preferences, muted session identifiers, and an encryption key on your device so notifications can work.

Local Storage

Motif may store app settings on your device, including configured servers, terminal appearance preferences, quick commands, push notification preferences, speech recognition credentials, and cached connection state. You can remove this data by deleting the app or clearing app data where the operating system allows it.

Third-Party Services

Motif may communicate with the following services when you use related features:

  • Apple services, including App Store services and Apple Push Notification service.
  • The Motif server that you configure and operate, or a review/demo server that you choose to connect to.
  • A speech recognition service when you use voice input.
  • Tailscale or other network transport services when you configure those connection modes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share app data with advertisers or data brokers. Data is shared only as needed to provide app features, such as connecting to your server, delivering push notifications, or performing speech recognition.

Children

Motif is a developer tool and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as Motif changes. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date on this page.
